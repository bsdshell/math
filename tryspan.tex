Let $\vec{e}_i = (0,\dots,0,1,0,\dots,0)'$ be an $n\times 1$ column 
vector with $1$ in the $i$-th position and $0$ in all others. Then 
$\SPAN\{\vec{e}_1,\vec{e}_2,\dots,\vec{e}_n\} = \mathbb{R}^n$.
